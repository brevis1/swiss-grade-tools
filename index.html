2. The Traditional Swiss Grading System

Switzerland uses a 6-point grading scale, where 6.0 indicates excellence and 1.0 represents the lowest performance. The passing grade is typically 4.0.

GradeDescriptionOutcome
6.0ExcellentOutstanding achievement
5.5–5.0Very GoodAbove average
4.5–4.0SatisfactoryPass
<4.0FailImprovement required

While accurate, the manual process required teachers to compute averages by hand, often delaying feedback for students.

5. Calculating Grades with Tools

Swiss grade calculation typically uses simple or weighted averages:

Simple Average: (Grade₁ + Grade₂ + Grade₃ + …) ÷ n
Example: (5.0 + 4.5 + 5.5) ÷ 3 = 5.0
Weighted Average: (Grade₁×Weight₁ + Grade₂×Weight₂ + …) ÷ Total Weights
Example: (5.5×2 + 5.0×3 + 4.5×1) ÷ 6 = 5.08
